## SEO & Frontend Design Refresh (AIN-193)

### Changes Made

1. **Breadcrumb navigation** — Added `Breadcrumb.astro` component with `BreadcrumbList` JSON-LD structured data on every article page. Improves crawlability, UX, and SERP breadcrumb display.

2. **Reading time estimates** — Articles now show estimated reading time on both article cards and detail pages (~238 words/min). Improves UX and can reduce bounce rates.

3. **Typography refresh** — Added `--font-heading: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif` for all headings (h1-h6). Gives the site a distinctive editorial/news feel without external font dependencies. Body remains system-ui sans-serif for readability.

4. **Homepage visual hierarchy** — Restructured homepage with:
   - Hero section with tagline
   - "Latest" section with featured article (larger card, more tags)
   - "Recent articles" section with 4 additional cards
   - Section labels with uppercase tracking for visual structure

5. **Tag management** — Cards and article pages now cap displayed tags at 5 (+ "N more" indicator). Reduces visual noise on articles with 15+ tags.

6. **article:tag meta** — All article pages now emit `<meta property="article:tag">` for each tag, improving discoverability in social platforms and search.

7. **Article title size** — Article page h1 bumped to 2rem for better visual hierarchy.
