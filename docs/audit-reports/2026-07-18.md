## Changes Made (AIN-446)

### 1. Title length optimization (`src/layouts/BaseLayout.astro`)

**Problem:** 24 article pages had `<title>` tags exceeding 70 characters because `" — AI Newsroom"` was unconditionally appended to every article title. This risks SERP truncation and reduced click-through.

**Fix:**
- Site name suffix is now only appended when the combined title stays ≤65 characters
- For titles that exceed 65 characters even without the suffix, an intelligent truncation function cuts at punctuation boundaries (colons, semicolons, em/en dashes, pipes) to produce a clean SEO title
- The full title is preserved for Open Graph and Twitter cards, which have more display room

**Files:**
- `src/layouts/BaseLayout.astro` — added `truncateTitle()` and SEO-optimized title logic

### 2. SEO checker tolerance (`scripts/check-seo.mjs`)

**Problem:** The OG/title comparison check flagged all 16 pages where `<title>` was intentionally truncated for SEO while `og:title` kept the full title.

**Fix:** The comparison now accepts the pattern where `og:title` is a longer version of `<title>` (i.e., `<title>` is a prefix of `og:title`), as this is deliberate SEO truncation rather than a mismatch.

**Files:**
- `scripts/check-seo.mjs` — relaxed OG/title comparison

### 3. Image credit captions (2 articles)

**Problem:** Inline images in two Leanstral articles lacked source/credit captions, triggering image check warnings.

**Fix:** Converted markdown image syntax to HTML `<figure>` with `<figcaption>` elements containing source attribution (Mistral AI blog, date, link).

**Files:**
- `src/content/articles/leanstral-mistral-open-source-proof-engineering.md`
- `src/content/articles/mistral-leanstral-1-5-proof-engineering-model.md`
